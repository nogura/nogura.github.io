At Nogura we offer Asian fusion dishes, always looking for a harmonious balance between different flavors and innovative techniques.

Mission

We are a fusion restaurant where traditional Mediterranean and Asian cuisine come together, seeking a harmonious balance of flavors and techniques to innovate in the two cultures. In addition, in the development of our work, we seek to raise awareness and reduce our environmental impact.

Vision

Our aim is to be a reference in the environmental field within a period of 3 years, through significant waste reduction, the use of food for the preparation of other dishes or decorations, and the use of renewable energy.

Values

Not only do we care about giving you better quality products, we also want to be respectful of the world while reducing our environmental impact. In our kitchen we follow the Zero Waste philosophy, where we try not to create non-recyclable waste by reducing its use or using other alternatives.

So we use renewable energy together with the SomEnergia cooperative and we clean the whole restaurant with organic products.
